### About me

**I'm Fabian, lifelong Sci-Fi enthusiast curious about new worlds from Hesse, Germany.**

Currently in my late 20s, working in public transportation as a project manager for customer service and as a public transport safety advocate.

I studied urban planning at a university in the centre of Germany, specialising in sustainable regional development and mobility planning during my master’s degree.

### The idea behind the Xenos Social Club

Since I was a child, I had the dream of a real life Sci-Fi hangout inspired by the likes of Mos Eisley Cantina and Quark's Bar. Imagine an immersive space where anyone, from interstellar traders to curious humans, can come together and share a story or a drink.

While building a real-life hobby space might be a bit ambitious for now, this website is set to be my creative corner to share stories and ideas with fellow fans.

> Disclaimer: I made this site mostly for fun, learning how to build a page with GitHub and grow it along the way. If you’ve wandered here by chance, welcome! It’s not fancy, but it works, and that’s what matters.
